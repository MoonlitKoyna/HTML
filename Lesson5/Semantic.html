Facts about Dogs

Dogs are truly the most precious companions, radiating love and loyalty like sunshine on a sleepy Sunday morning. Did you know that every dog has a unique nose print — just like a fingerprint? It’s their little identity badge, hidden in plain sight on that boopable snoot. Puppies dream just like we do, often twitching or making tiny barks while they chase imaginary butterflies in their sleep. Dogs are also deeply emotional; they can sense when you're feeling down and will quietly curl up beside you like a fluffy emotional support cloud. When they run around in wild little circles — called “zoomies” — that’s just pure joy overflowing. Even their tails talk: a wag to the right means they’re happy, while a wag to the left might mean they’re a bit unsure. And though people say dogs see in black and white, they can actually see soft blues and yellows — like living in a vintage pastel dream. Some dogs even carry around their favorite toys like teddy bears, showing how deeply they form emotional bonds. From their soft ears to their big hearts, dogs are pure magic wrapped in fur and love.

Honestly? Dogs are not just pets — they’re living, breathing comfort spells
